What is the importance of formulas and functions in Advanced Excel? +

Formulas and functions are essential components of Advanced Excel because they automate calculations and improve data accuracy. Popular functions include VLOOKUP, XLOOKUP, INDEX MATCH, IF, SUMIFS, COUNTIFS, and TEXT Functions. These functions allow users to perform complex calculations, retrieve information, analyze data, and automate repetitive tasks. This reduces manual effort and minimizes errors. Businesses use formulas extensively for financial analysis, budgeting, reporting, payroll processing, inventory management, and performance tracking. By mastering formulas and functions, students gain powerful tools that significantly improve productivity and problem-solving capabilities.
